Why it isn't a wrapper

The forecast's own
confidence bands lie.

An 80% band should contain reality 80% of the time. On real urea backtests it caught just 22%. Acting on raw bands means acting on false certainty. Fixing that is our core.

  • 🎯Conformal recalibration — rebuild bands from hindcast error so 80% means 80%.
  • ⚖️Trust score — down-weight forecasts the model can't get right.
  • ⚡Adaptive — shift any assumption mid-run; the decision visibly re-solves.
